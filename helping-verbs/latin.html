Out of her mind and terrified, with a timid path, the sister heard [this], defiling her face with her nails and her chest with her fists. Were that pyre and the fires and altars preparing this [thing], this [thing], for me? What should I complain about first, having been deserted? Did you, dying, scorn your comrade [and] sister?
