Me

My name is Edward Wang and I am currently an eighth grader at Basis Flagstaff. I have two sisters and I live in a suburban area in Flagstaff. I won 2nd place in the 2015 Coconino County Spelling Bee. I also won 3rd place in the 2018 NAU Piano Individual Contest and 1st place in the 2019 NAU Individual Contest and 1st in the 2019 NAU Concerto Contest. I am also able to speak Chinese (汉语) , Japanese (日本語), Korean (한국), German (Deutsche), and Navajo (Diné Bizaad) to varying degrees.
